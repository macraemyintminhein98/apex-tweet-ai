# NicheTweet AI

NicheTweet AI is an AI-powered tweet scheduler designed for niche accounts. Provide prompts related to your niche, and our AI will generate and schedule unique tweets, ensuring a consistent and engaging Twitter presence without manual daily effort.

### Features:
- AI-powered tweet generation.
- Automated scheduling to Twitter.
- Focus on specific niches (e.g., survivalist tips).
